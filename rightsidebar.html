What is LitRater?

'Lit' is for the hundreds of free short stories uploaded by authors and readers alike.

'Rater' is for the ratings that will help drive a recommendation engine based on the same math as other successful models.

Together it is LitRater a site where you can upload your original work and read everything from hundred year old classics to fan-fiction written yesterday. All on the comfort of your e-device and all for free.
